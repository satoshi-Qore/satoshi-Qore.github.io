Curiosity about what happens inside decentralized networks led me to QoreChain. I now channel that into concrete artifacts: monitoring tools with no external dependencies, bilingual guides that don't assume prior knowledge, and runbooks that help operators respond calmly when nodes go down. I also research how post-quantum cryptographic primitives could be integrated into live blockchain protocols.
